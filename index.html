Imagine this: your mother opens the front door. Her daughter is standing there — smiling, holding flowers. But your mother's eyes are empty. Not cold. Not unkind. Just… searching.

She knows this person matters. She can feel it in her chest. But the name, the relationship, the last time they spoke — it's gone. And in that silence, both of them feel the loss.

MemoryLens exists so that moment never has to be one of shame. A gentle overlay quietly whispers the answer: "This is Priya. Your daughter. She visited two days ago."

Passive. Silent. Always On.

The patient does nothing. The system does everything.

Designed for clarity, not distraction

The overlay uses large, readable text in calm colors. No sound. No intrusive alert. Information appears only when the system is confident in the match — and always positioned beside the face, never on top of it.

The patient never has to ask "Who are you?" The answer is already there — quiet, dignified, and private.
